February 3-4, 2017

Get ready for the first Make Zurich hackathon ever!

What is it?

We are bringing together the local hacker/maker community and the city administration to explore new ways of solving the problems of our city with the help of open networks and civic tech.

WHEN?

Kick-off & apero January 27, 2017
Open Lab January 28 - February 2, 2017
Hackathon February 3-4, 2017

WHERE?

The event will take place in Zurich city, the specific location will be announced very soon!.
